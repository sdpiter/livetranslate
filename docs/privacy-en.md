# LiveTranslate — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2025‑MM‑DD

## Summary
LiveTranslate processes audio and text locally on your device. We do not send your audio or translations to servers.

## Processed data
- Microphone and/or system audio is processed on-device for speech recognition and translation. It is not transmitted or stored after processing.
- Subtitle history is kept in memory only for the current session.

## Collected data
To improve stability we may collect:
- Crash and performance data (device model, OS version, app version, error stack traces).
- A technical installation identifier (not linked to identity).
We do not collect audio, recognized text, or personal information.

## Use of data
- Diagnostics, bug fixing, performance improvements.

## Sharing and storage
- Diagnostic data may be processed by crash reporting services (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) over TLS.

## Permissions
- Microphone — speech recognition.
- Draw over other apps — subtitle overlay.
- Screen/audio capture — only with your explicit consent, to subtitle currently playing content.  
The app does not record phone calls and does not work with DRM‑protected content.

## Your choices
- Disable telemetry in app settings.
- Clear model cache in settings.
